Committee Jurisdiction

The Land Use Committee reviews Special Permit and Site Plan Approval petitions; utility petitions; zone change petitions; sign permits; lodging and dormitory licenses; automobile dealer licensing; towed vehicle storage permits; and Community Preservation housing proposals.
